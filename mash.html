WELCOME TO THE FORGOTTEN GAME OF YOUR CHILDHOOD: MASH.

PREMISE: Ever wanted to dream about your future? Plan your wedding, choose your car, etc. Mash is the game that preceded The Sims (TM, E.A.), back when all we had to cure boredom in study hall or while waiting for the bus in the auditorium was a sheet of looseleaf and a couple of giggly friends.

(MASH stands for Mansion, Apartment, Street, House)

HOW TO PLAY: you must create categories, and then choices for those categories. Click on Add More Detail to add categories, and then add choices for each category (it should show up after you type in something.) Then results will be displayed when you click the Future button.
